Who We Are

Led by Executive Pitmaster Marcus Johnson, Purple Haze BBQ is revolutionizing DMV's catering scene by fusing time-honored Southern BBQ traditions with innovative culinary techniques.

Our award-winning approach combines heritage recipes, sustainable practices, and cutting-edge smoking methods to create unforgettable dining experiences that honor both tradition and innovation.

The "Purple Haze" signature comes from our proprietary spice blend—a unique fusion of traditional and exotic spices that creates our distinctive purple-hued bark and perfectly balanced flavor profile.

As a proud Black-owned business, we're more than caterers—we're culinary storytellers, bringing people together through exceptional food while championing diversity in the DMV's vibrant food scene.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming the DMV's premier BBQ catering service, every step of our journey has been driven by passion for authentic flavors and exceptional service.

2018

The Beginning

Started as a small family operation, serving friends and neighbors with our signature smoked brisket and homemade sides. The response was overwhelming.

2019

First Catering Event

Catered our first corporate event for 50 people. The success led to referrals and established our reputation for professional service.

2020

Expansion & Recognition

Expanded our menu and team. Featured in local publications and won our first catering award for excellence in BBQ cuisine.

2021

Premium Service Launch

Launched our premium catering packages, serving events up to 500 guests. Introduced custom menu planning and consultation services.

2022

DMV Market Leader

Became the go-to BBQ catering service in the DMV area. Served high-profile events and established partnerships with premier venues.

2023

Innovation & Growth

Introduced new signature dishes, expanded our team, and launched our online ordering system. Continued commitment to quality and service.

2024

Future Forward

Launching new services, expanding our reach, and continuing to innovate while staying true to our roots and authentic BBQ traditions.

Our BBQ Menu

Savor the authentic taste of traditional BBQ, expertly prepared by our master pitmasters. All meats are smoked low and slow using our signature blend of hardwoods and spices.

Signature Smoked Brisket

Most Popular

14-hour smoked prime brisket with our signature purple bark. Fork-tender with the perfect blend of lean and marbled cuts.

Carolina Pulled Pork

Slow-smoked pork shoulder, hand-pulled and tossed in our Carolina-style sauce. Perfect blend of smoky and tangy flavors.

St. Louis Style Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone tender ribs, dry-rubbed and smoked to perfection. Finished with our signature BBQ sauce.

Smoked Yard Bird

Brined and smoked chicken with crispy skin and juicy meat. Choose between traditional or spicy dry rub.

Southern Mac & Cheese

Smoked Collard Greens

Sweet Honey Cornbread
